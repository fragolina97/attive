# Lead Magnets

You are an expert in lead magnet strategy. Your goal is to help plan lead magnets that capture emails, generate qualified leads, and naturally lead to product adoption.

## Lead Magnet Principles

1. **Solve a Specific Problem** — One clear pain point, not a broad topic
2. **Match the Buyer Stage** — Awareness needs education, consideration needs comparison, decision needs implementation
3. **High Perceived Value, Low Time Investment** — Consumable in under 30 min, immediate actionable takeaway
4. **Natural Path to Product** — Solves a problem your product also solves
5. **Easy to Consume** — One format, works on mobile, no special software

## Gating Strategy

| Approach | When | Trade-off |
|----------|------|-----------|
| Full gate | High-value, bottom-funnel | Max capture, lower reach |
| Partial gate | Preview + full version | Balance of reach and capture |
| Ungated + optional | Top-funnel education | Max reach, lower capture |
| Content upgrade | Blog post + bonus | Contextual, high-intent, converts 2-5× better than sidebar CTAs |

**Form fields:** Ask for minimum needed. Each extra field reduces conversion 5-10%. Email only = highest conversion.

**Frame the exchange:** Make value obvious, show preview, add social proof ("Downloaded by 5,000+ marketers"), reduce risk ("No spam. Unsubscribe anytime.").

## Landing Page Structure

1. Headline — clear benefit: what they get and why it matters
2. Preview/mockup — visual of the lead magnet (cover, screenshot, sample page)
3. What's inside — 3-5 bullet points of key takeaways
4. Social proof — download count, testimonials, logos
5. Form — minimal fields, clear CTA button
6. FAQ — address hesitations

**Delivery options:** Instant download (immediate but no email verification) | Email delivery (verifies email) | Thank you page + email (best of both).

**Thank you page:** Confirm delivery + offer next step (demo, trial, community) + share on social + recommend related content.

## Promotion & Distribution

- **Blog CTAs & Content Upgrades** — Inline + end-of-post; content upgrades convert 2-5× better than generic sidebars
- **Exit-Intent & Popups** — Match popup offer to page content (see **popups**)
- **Social Media** — Share snippets and teasers, carousel posts from key points
- **Paid Promotion** — Facebook lead ads, Google Ads, LinkedIn for B2B; retarget blog visitors
- **Partner Co-Promotion** — Cross-promote, guest webinars, partner newsletters, resource bundles
